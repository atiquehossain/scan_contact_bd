The Privacy Promise

We built NoNumQR on a simple principle: your utility should not cost you your identity. Our infrastructure is designed from the ground up to facilitate necessary communication while actively severing the link to your personal data.

qr_code

Only a public URL is inside the QR.

Anyone scanning your code accesses an ephemeral routing link. There is zero personal data encoded within the pattern itself.

no_sim
phonelink_lock

Your phone number stays private.

Communication is proxied through our secure servers. The person contacting you never sees your number, and you never see theirs.

How We Protect Your Data

vpn_key

Token-Protected Conversations

Every scan generates a unique, time-limited cryptographic token. This token authorizes a temporary communication bridge. Once the bridge is closed or expires, the connection is permanently severed.

data_alert

Minimal Data Retention

We do not store the content of your proxy conversations. Message metadata is retained only for 24 hours to ensure delivery and facilitate abuse reporting, after which it is systematically purged.

visibility_off

Anonymity by Default

Your account requires only a valid email and phone number for routing. We do not construct advertising profiles, track your location, or sell meta-data to third-party brokers.

folder_managed

Data Export & Deletion

You maintain absolute ownership of your account data. You can request a complete export of your routing history or initiate a permanent, unrecoverable account deletion at any time.

gavel

Reporting Abuse

While we ensure anonymity, we do not tolerate harassment. If a caller violates our terms, you can instantly block their device fingerprint and report the interaction to our trust and safety team.
