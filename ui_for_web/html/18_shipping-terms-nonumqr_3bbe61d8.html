Shipping & Delivery Policy

We employ a strict Cash on Delivery (COD) model to ensure you only pay when your physical QR package is safely in your hands. Review our streamlined dispatch process below.

check_circle

1. Order Confirmation

Upon placing an order, our system immediately verifies the details. You will receive an automated confirmation SMS to the registered number. No payment is required at this stage.

print

2. Secure Printing

Your unique, anonymized QR code is generated securely. It is then printed onto our durable, weather-resistant materials in a controlled facility, ensuring the linkage to your privacy vault remains uncompromised prior to activation.

local_shipping

3. COD Dispatch & Tracking

The package is handed over to our logistics partners. You will receive a tracking link via SMS. Payment is collected in cash or via authorized digital terminals exclusively upon successful delivery at your specified address.
